Tests for viewport-units-buggyfill.js

  • vw test: with buggyfill and without. Without buggyfill, iOS Safari will fail when rotating the device.
  • vh test: with buggyfill and without. Without buggyfill, iOS Safari will fail when rotating the device.
  • vmin test: with buggyfill and without. Without buggyfill, iOS Safari will fail when rotating the device and IE9 will just fail.
  • vmax test: with buggyfill and without. Without buggyfill, iOS Safari will fail when rotating the device and IE9 and 10 will just fail.
  • viewport units in calc() statement: with buggyfill and without. Without buggyfill, fails in iOS Safari.
  • vmax units in calc() statement: with buggyfill and without. Without buggyfill, fails in iOS Safari and IE9 and 10.
  • viewport units in IE Visual Filters: with buggyfill and without. Without buggyfill, text shadows do not grow with the viewport height in IE9.
